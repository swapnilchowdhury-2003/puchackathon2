Highly Decorated & Globally Recognised

The Premier University Computer Club (PUCC), operating under the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) in Chattogram, is highly decorated for its accomplishments in competitive programming, hackathons, and tech innovation. From qualifying for the world's most prestigious programming contests to winning international space-tech challenges, PUCC continues to set new benchmarks of excellence for student organisations across Bangladesh.
