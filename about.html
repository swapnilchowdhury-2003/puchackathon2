Our History & Vision

The Premier University Computer Club (PUCC) is one of the most active and prestigious student organizations at Premier University, established in 2015 under the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). Officially approved by the Honorable Vice-Chancellor in 2020, the club continues to play a vital role in nurturing technical talent across the university.

PUCC currently operates with over 350–360 active members each semester, and has proudly connected more than 1,500 students throughout its history. We are structured into six specialized wings, each focused on developing essential skills and fostering innovation.

Our mission is to create a collaborative learning environment where students can explore technology, solve real-world problems, and prepare for both academic and professional success. We conduct regular bootcamps, workshops, seminars, and competitions—open to students from all disciplines, including those outside the CSE department. PUCC stands as a bridge between classroom learning and practical experience, committed to empowering the next generation of tech leaders.
